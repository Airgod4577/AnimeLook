Description

An ordinary student, Taiju, tells his genius friend Senku about his plans to confess to his beloved Yuzuriha. They meet under a tree, but suddenly a strange light illuminates everything around. Taiju pushes Yuzuriha under a tree and shields her, but the light intensifies and turns everyone to stone. Humanity has stopped, traces of civilization have begun to disappear, but Taiju retains his sanity, motivating himself to save Yuzuriha. Ultimately, Taiju manages to get out of the stone. He learns that Senku has also managed to shed the stone shell, counting every second of his existence. At the same time, it turns out that it is now October 5, 5738 in the world.

characters

Ishigami Senku (石いし神がみ千空 Ishigami Senku:?) is the protagonist of Doctor Stone. He is friends with Taiju and Yuzuriha. Senku is known for his catchphrases, one of which is "Ten billion percent" [8]. He emerged from the petrification six months before Taiju, and eventually created the stone formula with the help of Taiju [9]. In the stone world, Senku wants to restore civilization and create the Realm of Science. He became the champion of the village games and currently holds the title of village chief in Ishigami Village, where he was widely known due to the influence of Byakuya, the founder of the village, who passed down the tale of Senku through the generations. An experienced fighter, in the world of the past known as one of the strongest. Able to deal with monsters with his bare hands, but does not waste his skills due to his calm nature and thoughtfulness. Tsukasa disagrees with Senku's idea of ​​bringing everyone back to life. Dreams of a world in which the most worthy representatives of humanity will live.
